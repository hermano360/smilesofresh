We make every effort to value your time and we schedule your appointment time just for you. We do our best to accommodate your wishes.

However, some times are more requested than others. We are usually most busy after school, school vacation and holidays.

When you reserve the time, it means this is your time and cannot be used by someone else.

When you fail to keep the appointment, we can't use the time you reserved, and someone else who may have wanted that time can't use it either.

There is a $40 fee for each missed appointment.

To avoid this fee, we appreciate your courtesy of giving us at least a 24-hour notice if you have a conflict with your appointment, and we will be happy to reschedule for a different day or time. Thank you.
